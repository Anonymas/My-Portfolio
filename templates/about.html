About Me

I’m a software engineer based in Kenya with a strong interest in building practical digital solutions that solve real-world problems. My work sits at the intersection of software development, cloud technologies, and workflow automation.

I began my journey studying Computer Science at The Catholic University of Eastern Africa, where I developed a solid foundation in programming, systems design, and problem solving. Along the way, I became increasingly interested in how software can streamline processes, improve efficiency, and power modern digital infrastructure.

Since then, I’ve worked on a variety of projects including healthcare workflow systems, cloud-hosted web applications, and automated backend processes. My experience spans full-stack development, API integrations, database design, and deploying applications on cloud platforms such as AWS.

Through freelance work and collaborative projects, I’ve helped design and implement systems that automate manual workflows, integrate third-party services like payments and messaging APIs, and improve operational efficiency for organizations.

Beyond building software, I’m continuously expanding my knowledge in cloud computing and cybersecurity. I believe the future of technology lies in building secure, scalable systems that can adapt to rapidly evolving digital environments.

This space documents my work, projects, and ongoing learning as I continue exploring new technologies and building solutions that make a meaningful impact.
